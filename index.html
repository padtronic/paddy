Paddy's Electrical Shop Lubao

Paddy’s  electrical shop is a specialized establishment that deals
with various aspects of electrical equipment, components, and services.

1.Purpose and Scope:

Paddy’s Electrical shop serves as a hub for all things related to electricity.
It caters to both residential and commercial needs.
The shop provides a wide range of products, including wiring cables, lighting fixtures,
switches, circuit breakers, transformers, and other electrical components.

2.Products and Services:

Electrical Components:


The shop stocks a variety of electrical components, such as wires, cables, connectors, and junction boxes.

Lighting Fixtures:

Customers can find an array of lighting options, from bulbs and LED strips and outdoor lamps.

Switches and Outlets:

The shop offers different types of switches, sockets, and outlets for homes and offices.

Safety Devices:

Circuit breakers, surge protectors, and grounding equipment are essential safety components available here.

Tools and Accessories:

Electricians and DIY enthusiasts can purchase tools like pliers, hammer, screwdrivers, wire strippers, and phase testers.

Appliances and Appliances Repair:

Paddy’s electrical shop also sell small appliances e.g.,cooking coils, fans, and water heaters. We offer repair services too.

3.Expertise and Assistance:

-Our Knowledgeable staff assist customers in selecting the right products based on their requirements.
-Electricians often visit the shop for specialized advice and to purchase materials for specific projects.

4.Safety and Compliance:

-The shop ensures that all products meet safety standards and comply with local regulations.
-Staff educate customers about safe installation practices and proper usage of electrical equipment.

5.Customer Experience:

-A well-organized layout allows customers to browse products easily.
-Excellent customer service, clear signage, and informative displays enhance the overall experience.

6.Location and Accessibility:
